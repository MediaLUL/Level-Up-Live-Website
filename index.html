About Level Up Live Entertainment

The flagship operation bringing the Level Up Live platform to life.

Level Up Live Entertainment (LUL-E) is the venue and operations arm behind the first-of-its-kind immersive shooting experience—fueled by the proprietary tech platform, Level Up Live (LUL).

While LUL develops the smart-target technology, game systems, scoring engines, and backend infrastructure, LUL-E is the entity that brings these innovations to market through physical locations, beginning with our flagship facility in Los Angeles.

Visit Level Up Live

This site is designed for investors interested in LUL-E—the business that builds, operates, and scales Level Up-powered venues for the public. It's the face, the atmosphere, and the operational engine behind the LUL experience. And it's what you're investing in.

Inside the Venue

Immersive gameplay. Real gear. Social energy.

Step into a next-gen shooting arena built for thrill. Move, aim, and fire at smart targets under reactive 3D projections—with live scoring and leaderboards keeping the pressure on. Designed for flow, capacity, and high guest satisfaction, the venue is built around a multi-zone experience that includes:

  • Shooting Zone – The heart of the action: 6 immersive shooting stations facing a smart target wall with projection-mapped visuals and responsive scoring.
  • Retail Zone – Branded merchandise, gear upgrades, and take-home souvenirs.
  • Food & Beverage Lounge – A bar and lounge area with craft drinks, snacks, and meals for celebrating or regrouping between rounds.
  • Event & Social Space – Configurable for birthdays, company offsites, group events, and competitions.

The Facility Engine. Built for Throughput. Powered by Play.

Every guest, every round, every reload—driving value.

Each Level Up Live facility is a high-throughput venue designed for repeat play, fast rotations, and big smiles. Our floorplan supports:

6 Zones × 6 Stations per Zone × 4 Players per Station
Up to 144 guests active simultaneously

Players rotate through mission-driven zones while scoring points, competing in multiplayer formats, and fueling up between games. With average costs of goods per guest under $1.20 and gross profit margins of up to 98%, each round delivers adrenaline and returns.
